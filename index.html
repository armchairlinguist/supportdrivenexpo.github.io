    July 22, 2017

    Portland, Oregon

    SDX is...

    • A 1-day event with workshops, talks, and more to suit different learning styles

    • Focused on learning and developing technical and nontechnical skills

    • Taught from a Support perspective, so you can apply skills right away

    • The first conference of its kind to focus on individual contributors in Support

    We set out to create an event that works for everyone in Support - from folks on the front lines to managers and up. The case for ROI is simple: if one of these sessions could benefit your career or your job, you should be here. (P.S. - we’re looking for volunteers!)

    Venue

    SDX will be hosted at Portland State University’s Smith Memorial Student Union on July 22nd. For one weekend, we’ll turn Portland into Supportland and we’d love to have you be a part of it!

    Here are a few reasons we’re excited for SDX to be at Smith Union:

    • Multiple rooms for workshops and talks. Choose from a variety of workshops and talks that best suit what you want to learn.

    • Quick and easy transit between rooms. It’s all in one building, so there’ll be plenty of time to get to where you want to go.

    • A beautiful, large space for sponsors and the GIF battle. We’ve got ideas for bigger and better ways for sponsors to be a part of SDX and we’ll have the space for it.

    • Located just south of downtown Portland. Whether you’re into food, beer, cycling, weird things - SDX will be the perfect time to visit Portland.

    Official Hotel for SDX

    We’ve created a room block at the University Place Hotel near SDX. The room block helps you get the best hotel rate, and creates a fun place for chance encounters and meetups, as fellow attendees are all nearby!

    The block rate is $111/night (+ 15.3% tax). This rate is for July 21st and July 22nd. Hot breakfast and wifi is included in the room rate.

    In order to block this special rate, please call the hotel at (503) 221-0140 or 1 (866) 845-4647, and as for the “Support Driven” room block.

    This special rate is available until May 21st, 2017.
